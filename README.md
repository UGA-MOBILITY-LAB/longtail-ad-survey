# From Generation to Guarantee — Survey Companion Site

Interactive literature hub for the survey **"From Generation to Guarantee: A Survey on World Models, Vision-Language-Action Models, and Safety Verification for Long-Tail Autonomous Driving"** (Haohua Que, Qianyi Wu, Handong Yao — College of Engineering, University of Georgia; manuscript under review, 2026).

**Live site:** https://uga-mobility-lab.github.io/longtail-ad-survey/

## What's here

A zero-dependency static site presenting the 302 works covered by the survey:

- **Survey axis** — the generation → understanding → guarantee pipeline with its closed loop
- **Paper Explorer** — interactive scatter chart (year × research area; marker shape = venue type, colour = area) with filter dropdowns, search, and live counts
- **Statistics** — per-area and per-year distributions, computed at runtime from the bibliography data
- **Paper list** — collapsible per-area tables with arXiv / Scholar links

All data in `data.js` is extracted directly from the survey bibliography; no entries are hand-edited.

## Cite

> Que, H., Wu, Q., Yao, H. "From Generation to Guarantee: A Survey on World Models, Vision-Language-Action Models, and Safety Verification for Long-Tail Autonomous Driving." Manuscript under review, 2026.

No build step. Clone and open `index.html` in a browser.
