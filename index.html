From Generation to Guarantee

A Survey on World Models, Vision-Language-Action Models, and Safety Verification for Long-Tail Autonomous Driving

Haohua Que, Qianyi Wu, Handong Yao · College of Engineering, University of Georgia

302 papers 7 research areas 1989–2025 coverage

Citation

For use in publications and presentations please cite this survey as follows:

Que, H., Wu, Q., Yao, H. “From Generation to Guarantee: A Survey on World Models, Vision-Language-Action Models, and Safety Verification for Long-Tail Autonomous Driving.” Manuscript under review, 2026.

The Survey Axis

The survey reads three research families as one coordinated response to the open-world long-tail challenge: world models generate the tail, vision-language-action models understand it, and safety verification guarantees behaviour on it.
